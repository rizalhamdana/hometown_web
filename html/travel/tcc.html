Tanjungpinang City Center



Tanjung Pinang City Center is the newest and biggest shopping mall in the town of Tanjung Pinang. The mall houses myraid food and beverage, fashion, accessories, gadgets and entertainment tenants to provide an integrated shopping experience. Opened in 2016, it has three levels of retail shops including the Matahari departmental store and a basement where the supermarket (Hypermart) is located. One of the must-buys of Indonesia-Kueh Lapis can be found here. The mall is especially popular amongst the locals as it hosts the city's only cinema (Cinema XXI).

With facilities such as the indoor playground and fun mini vehicle or animal rides available for rent, the little ones will never get bored in this mall. Catch the weekend events such as retail sales, bridal make up contests, karate championship, colouring activities for children as well as festive programs accompanied with live music!

10:00am - 10:00pm
