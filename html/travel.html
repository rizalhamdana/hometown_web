Natures

Mangrove Forest Tour

The one hour tour takes you on a nature encounter with Bintan's rich mangrove forests.

Treasure Bay

Treasure Bay is a 338-hectare waterfront resort city located on the Indonesian island of Bintan.

Culture

Roti Jala

Roti Jala is a popular Malay tea time snack served with curry dishes which can be found in Indonesia, Malaysia and Singapore.

Zapin Melayu

Zapin is a Malay dance form that is popular Indonesia, especially in Malay-populated provinces in Sumatera

Gurindam

Gurindam is a type of irregular verse forms of traditional Malay poetry.

Tanjungpinang International Dragon Boat Race 2018

On 19th to 21st October 2018, the Tanjung Pinang International Dragon Boat 2018 race is set to focus the spotlight on the city of Tanjung Pinang.

History and Landmark Places

Gedung Gonggong

Gedung Gonggong serves as a Tourism Infomation Centre and is the icon of Tanjung Pinang.

Monumen Raja Haji Fisabilillah

The monument is situated on the seafront with a very strategic position and the beautiful scenery around.

Penyengat Island

A small island situated across Tanjung Pinang, capital of the Riau Islands Province, the island of Penyengat in the 18th century was the twin-seat of the Sultan of the Johor-Riau Kingdom.

Vihara Ksitigarbha Boddhisattva

the Vihara, otherwise known as Vihara Patung Seribu (Vihara of a thousand statues), it is indeed a remarkable sight to behold.

Shopping

Tanjungpinang City Center

Tanjung Pinang City Center is the newest and biggest shopping mall in the town of Tanjung Pinang.

Rimba Jaya

Rimba Jaya is one of the best hangout spots in Tanjung Pinang after the sun sets.

Tanjungpinang Wet Markets

The traditional wet markets of the bustling city of Tanjung Pinang are treasure troves waiting to be discovered.
